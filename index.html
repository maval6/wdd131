Douala, Cameroon

Douala is the largest city in Cameroon and its economic capital, located on the Wouri River along the Atlantic coast. It is a bustling port city known for its vibrant trade, industrial activity, and cultural diversity. Douala serves as a major gateway to the country, hosting the largest port in Central Africa and Douala International Airport. Douala remains a dynamic hub for business and culture in the region.
