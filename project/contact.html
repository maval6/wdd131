Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the club?

You can join by clicking the "Become a Member" button and filling out the registration form.

Are your events free?

Most of our events are free for members. Some special workshops may have a nominal fee.

How can I contribute?

Members can contribute through our GitHub repository, writing blog posts, or leading workshops.

Do you offer mentorship?

Yes, we have a mentorship program that pairs experienced developers with newcomers.
